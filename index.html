Hello!

I am a 3rd year PhD candidate in the Global Seismology and Tectonics Lab Group at the University of Arizona Department of Geosciences with Dr. Susan Beck. My research is primarily centered around the South America subduction zone and the active tectonic processes responsible for the formation of the Andes through the lense of seismic imaging techniques. I spend most of my time in my office squinting at squiggles (seismograms), but I can also be found outside hiking, climbing, or running around Tucson.
