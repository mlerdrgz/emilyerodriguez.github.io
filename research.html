 Imaging the Nazca Slab

  

  



 Responses to Ridge Subduction

  I’m a second year seismology PhD student in the Global Seismology and Tectonics (GSAT) lab group at the University of Arizona Department of Geosciences under Dr. Susan Beck.

  I work using earthquakes to seismically image subduction zones across varying scales. The main focus of my research is understanding the processes that influence subducting slabs and their surrounding mantle, with an emphasis on how these processes connect with observations at the surface.
